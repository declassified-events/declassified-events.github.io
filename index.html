About Declassified Events

Declassified Events is a music events company based in the UK, specialising in underground electronic music. Founded in 2026, we aim to bring high-quality events featuring talented artists to music lovers across the country. Everyone at Declassified is a music lover through and through, and we aim to protect and nurture the scene that has given us all so much over the years.
