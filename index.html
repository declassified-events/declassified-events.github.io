About Us

The Declassified Collective is a community of multi-discplined artists & specialists dedicated to protecting and nurturing the UK underground scene that has given us all so much over the years. Founded in 2026, Declassified aims to bring high-quality events that center the community, establishments and artists that make the scene what it is.
